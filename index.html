The Catalyst Club

Chemistry in Motion:
Where Science Meets Innovation

The Catalyst Club is a student-driven organization dedicated to promoting chemistry education, fostering scientific curiosity, and creating opportunities for hands-on learning through interactive demonstrations and research.

ABOUT US

Our Pillars

Education

We provide hands-on learning experiences through workshops, lab sessions, and interactive demonstrations. Our goal is to make chemistry accessible and engaging for all students, regardless of their background.

Community

Join a vibrant community of chemistry enthusiasts. We foster collaboration, mentorship, and networking opportunities among students, faculty, and industry professionals.

Events

We make chemistry fun and accessible through public demos, hands-on activities, and interactive sessions. Our club sparks curiosity, supports learning, and connects students with seminars, guest lectures, and career opportunities in chemistry.

We provide assistance in the field of chemistry and encourage an atmosphere to exchange thoughts and ideas. Our events include seminars, visiting lecturers, and information about numerous job opportunities in chemistry.
